## Tips for Maximum Engagement:

1. **Post timing**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM (your timezone)
2. **Add a visual**: Screenshot of the app or before/after comparison
3. **Engage in comments**: Reply to everyone in the first hour
4. **Tag relevant people**: AI researchers, developer influencers, Next.js team
5. **Use 3-5 hashtags max**: Don't overdo it
6. **Add call-to-action**: "What feature would you add?" or "Would you use this?"
7. **Share in comments**: Add demo video or GIF in first comment
